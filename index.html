Disneyland,LA

About The Park

The gorgeous,fun and exciting Disneyland Park,located in Anaheim, California,USA was first opened on July 17, 1955.This is the only disney theme park buit to completion under the supervision on Walt Disney.It consists of various rides,snacks and shows. Here,you get to meet your favourite disney characters! You have to visit this lovely and charming place at least once in your life.

Foods

Cotton Candy And Lemonade

This drink is the perfect duo of a sweet cotton candy and a tangy lemonade.The cotton candy lemonade will surely refresh you after the long trip to the park.It's a must to try this.

Mickey Mouse Pretzel

This salty and fluffy snack will leave your lips smacking.The mickey pretzel is probably one of the best and cutest snacks you will find here.

Churros

These long ,fluffy and sweet snacks are a classic at the disneyland park.They taste toothsome let alone but the churros and chocolate syrup are one of the best combos.

Rides

Silly Symphony Swings

This fun ride lets you get a fresh breath of air by swinging you around.Each swing has two seats. So, you can enjoy this ride with your friend or a family member.

Goofy's Sky School

This thrilling ride might leave you trembling. The goofy's sky school is a mini roller coaster made for those who are brave and daring.You might need to wait for a while to ride this coaster as it usually has a pretty long line but the experience is worth it.

Luigi's Rollickin Roadsters

This goofy ride lets you sit on your favourite car character which drive around with the music. The "Luigi's Rollickin Roadsters" is one of the most lively rides at this park.

Personal Experience

I visited Disneyland with my family in the month June. Even though we didn't stay there for long,the experience was wholesome.The rides,food,shows,characters and everything there was very delightful. I recommend you to stay they for at least 2-3 days to have the best experience.I'll also suggest you to go there with your family and friends as they will make the visit even better.I have to admit, the visit to the disneyland park has been one of the most jolly experiences of my life.
